🔧 Troubleshooting

Having issues with D-Mail? Find solutions to common problems here.

☀️ 🌙

🔗 Connection Issues

❓ The OAuth authentication button is not working

Cause: Your browser may be blocking popups.

Solutions:

  • Disable the popup blocker in your browser
  • Right-click the button to copy the URL and paste it directly in your browser
  • Try a different browser

❓ "This app isn't verified" warning

Cause: This warning appears because the app is going through Google's verification process.

Solutions:

  1. Click "Advanced"
  2. Click "Go to [app name] (unsafe)"
  3. Continue with the authentication

❓ App password connection failed

Check the following:

  • Make sure IMAP is enabled in your email settings
  • Verify the app password was entered correctly (watch for spaces)
  • Ensure you are using the app password, not your account password
  • Two-factor authentication must be enabled to generate an app password

🔔 Notification Issues

❓ I'm not receiving any notifications

Check the following:

  • Make sure the bot's DMs are not blocked in your Discord settings
  • Verify your email account status is connected in /mail manage
  • Only new emails received after connecting will trigger notifications (not existing emails)

❓ Notifications are delayed

Cause: D-Mail checks for new emails every 30 seconds.

Normal range:

  • Typical: 30 seconds to 1 minute delay
  • Depending on server load, up to 2-3 minutes delay is possible

❓ I'm only receiving notifications for some emails

Possible causes:

  • The email may have been classified as spam
  • Mail server synchronization delay
  • If many emails arrive simultaneously, some may be missed

🔒 OAuth / Authentication Issues

❓ "Unable to verify Gmail credentials" error

Cause: The OAuth token has expired or the permissions have been revoked.

Solution:

  1. Delete the account in /mail manage
  2. Reconnect the same account using /mail add
  3. Complete the OAuth authentication again

❓ The bot is requesting re-authentication

Possible causes:

  • Google account password was changed
  • App permissions were revoked in your Google account settings
  • Token expired due to prolonged inactivity

Solution: Click the authentication button sent by the bot to re-authenticate.

✈ Send Email Issues

❓ "No connected email account" error

Cause: No Gmail account is connected.

Solution: Use /mail add to connect a Gmail account.

⚠
Email sending is only supported with Gmail accounts.

❓ Email sending failed

Check the following:

  • Verify the recipient email address is correct
  • Ensure attachments do not exceed 20MB total
  • Check if your OAuth authentication is still valid (you may need to reconnect)

❓ Attachments are not being added

Limitations:

  • Maximum of 8 files per email
  • Total attachment size must be under 20MB
  • Files can only be added in the DM channel where the compose panel is active

🤖 Bot Response Issues

❓ The bot is not responding

Check the following:

  • Verify the bot is online in your server
  • Make sure you are using the correct slash commands (/mail)
  • Use !ping to check the bot's status

❓ Buttons are not responding when clicked

Possible causes:

  • Old buttons from previous sessions may no longer work
  • Network connection issues
  • Try restarting the Discord app
