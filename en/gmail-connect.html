📧 Connect Gmail

Securely connect your Google account with OAuth. No password sharing required!

☀️ 🌙

🔒 What is OAuth?

OAuth is a method that allows you to grant app permissions without sharing your password.

✅
Benefits of Gmail OAuth:
  • No need to share your password with D-Mail
  • Revoke access anytime from your Google account
  • Only email read/send permissions are requested

📝 Gmail Connection Steps

Run the Mail Add Command

Type the following command to the D-Mail bot in Discord:

/mail add

Or you can click the "Connect Email" button.

Enter Your Gmail Address

Enter the Gmail address you want to connect in the popup window.

example@gmail.com

Entering an @gmail.com or @googlemail.com address will automatically proceed with the OAuth method.

Click the "Verify Email Ownership" Button

Click the "Verify Email Ownership" button in the message from the bot.

Clicking the button will redirect you to the Google login page.

Log in to Google

On the Google login page:

  1. Select the Gmail account you want to connect
  2. Review D-Mail's permission request
  3. Click the "Allow" button
⚠
You may see a "This app isn't verified" warning.
In this case, click "Advanced" → "Go to (unsafe)" to proceed.

Connection Complete!

After granting permissions, you'll be automatically redirected back to Discord and the connection will be complete.

✅
Connection complete! You will now receive Discord DM notifications when new Gmail messages arrive.

🔎 D-Mail Permissions

Permission Purpose
Read email messages To send notifications when new emails arrive
Send email Used when sending emails via the /mail compose feature
Email metadata access To display sender, subject, and other information
💡
D-Mail does not store your email content. Email data is deleted after sending the notification.

🔧 Troubleshooting

❓ I see an "App isn't verified" warning

This is a normal warning that appears during Google's app verification process. Click "Advanced" and then select "Go to (unsafe)" to proceed.

❓ The verification button isn't working

Popups may be blocked in your browser. Try disabling the popup blocker, or copy the button URL and paste it directly in your browser.

❓ I'm not receiving notifications after connecting

You won't receive notifications for existing emails right after connecting. Notifications will only be sent for new emails that arrive after the connection.
