❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is D-Mail? ▾
D-Mail is a Discord bot that delivers real-time email notifications. It supports Gmail OAuth and IMAP connections (Naver, Daum, iCloud, and more).
Which email services can I connect? ▾
Gmail connects securely via OAuth, while Naver/Daum/iCloud use IMAP with app passwords. See the Gmail guide and IMAP guide for details.
How often do notifications arrive? ▾
D-Mail checks for new emails every 30 seconds, so notifications typically arrive within 30 seconds to 1 minute.
How does verification code extraction work? ▾
Google Gemini AI analyzes the email content and extracts verification codes, displayed as Code: XXXXXX.
Is email content stored? ▾
D-Mail does not store email content. Email data is deleted after notifications are sent.
Can I connect multiple accounts? ▾
Yes. You can connect multiple email accounts. Notifications arrive in a single DM and indicate which account they belong to.
Do I get notifications for old emails after connecting? ▾
No. Notifications are sent only for new emails that arrive after you connect your account.
What should I do if I am not receiving notifications? ▾
Make sure Discord DMs are allowed and check the troubleshooting guide for common fixes.
