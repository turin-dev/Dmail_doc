⚙ Manage Email

View and manage your connected email accounts.

☀️ 🌙

💻 Open Management Panel

Use the following command to open the email management panel:

/mail manage

From the management panel, you can view all connected email accounts and remove them.

🗑 Delete an Email Account

Open Management Panel

/mail manage

Find the Account to Delete

Locate the email account you want to remove in the panel.

If you have many accounts, use the page navigation buttons to browse.

Click the ✕ Button

Click the ✕ button next to the account you want to delete.

⚠
Warning: Once deleted, you will no longer receive notifications for that account. You will need to reconnect the account to resume notifications.

Deletion Complete

The account is removed immediately, and the panel will update to reflect the change.

➕ Add a New Account

Click the "Connect Email" button in the management panel, or use the command:

/mail add

This will allow you to connect a new email account.

🔒 Privacy Information

D-Mail takes your privacy seriously and handles your data with care.

  • Email Masking: Email addresses are masked in the management panel for privacy. (e.g., u***r@gmail.com)
  • Token Encryption: OAuth tokens and app passwords are stored with encryption.
  • No Mail Content Storage: Email content is not stored after notifications are delivered.
  • Immediate Data Deletion: When you delete an account, all associated data is deleted immediately.
💡
For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

🔄 Reconnect an Account

If your OAuth authentication has expired or you are experiencing issues:

  1. Delete the existing account from the management panel
  2. Use /mail add to reconnect the same account
  3. Complete the OAuth authentication again
⚠
When reconnecting, your previous mail history will be reset. You will only receive notifications for new emails arriving after the reconnection.
